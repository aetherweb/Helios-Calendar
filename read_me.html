Installing Helios

Note: If your site already has any of the three files below do not upload the coresponding Helios files without renaming them. If you upload the entire zip you may overwrite your existing files which could break your site.

root index.php
/events directory
/admin directory
  1. Upload the contents of the Helios ZIP to your webserver.
  2. Create Your Helios database & database user account
    (user must have create & alter permissions)
  3. Rename globals_dist.php to globals.php and configure your Helios settings by editing your globals.php file in a text editor.
  4. Run the Helios web setup (You will be prompted for this by trying to open Helios in your browser)

Step by Step instructions are available in the Helios Documentation.

Upgrading Helios

NOTE: Do not copy the /events/index.php file or you will overwrite your existing Helios template and revert any customizations you've made.

It is highly recommended you make a backup of all files and your Helios database before upgrading.
  1. Upload the contents of the Helios Zip (all except the /events/setup directory) to your webserver.
  2. Upload /events/setup/upgrade.php to /events/upgrade.php
  3. Point your browser to YourHeliosInstall/events/upgrade.php and run the appropriate upgrade.
  4. Delete the upgrade.php file from your webserver.
Depending on the version you are using at the time of upgrade you may need to make small additions or changes to core Helios files by hand.
For details please visit the documentation.
